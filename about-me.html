About Me

Hi! Welcome to my studio! My love of textures and textile arts has grown exponentially in the past few years; from piecing cotton fabrics into classic quilts to combining tiny bits into collage quilts. The icing on the cake happened while living in Germany (with my USARMY Chaplain husband) when I was introduced to finishing my own quilts on a long arm quilting machine. Since then I have had the honor to learn and grow, stitching custom quilts on a variety of machines (APQS, Janome, Juki, Nolting). I currently finish quilts in my studio on an incredible Bernina Q24 long arm, with the sweet shine of Glide thread. Please make yourself at home and peek around at some quilty-eye-candy. Have questions? Need help deciding on how to finish your quilt? Contact me and we can chat. Bless your quilting!
